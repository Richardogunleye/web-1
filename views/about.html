about us

CropCityNG is one of the leading emerging cash-crop and perishable food product distribution companies in Lagos State, Nigeria, we cater to a diverse range of clients including domestic, commercial, and industrial sectors. Our business model revolves around purchasing cash crops and perishable products directly from farms in local communities at farm gate prices, transporting them to urban commercial centers, and selling them at affordable retail or wholesale prices depending on client needs. We pride ourselves on providing healthy, fresh products with minimized shelf life in our warehouse to ensure the best quality for our customers.

Our services also extend to offering warehouse space for other merchants and consultancy services for entrepreneurs looking to venture into the agro-value chain or for those conducting research. Sustainability and Relevance CropCityNG is dedicated to addressing the growing demand for food supply in Nigeria and Africa. By sourcing directly from farmers, we ensure that they receive a fair price for their products and facilitate a speedy sale, thus reducing post-harvest losses. This approach not only benefits the farmers by improving their income but also ensures that urban consumers receive fresh and affordable food products.
